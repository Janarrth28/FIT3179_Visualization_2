Australian Climate Change and Its Effects

This page explores how Australia’s changing climate relates to increasing bushfire activity. The map presents rainfall / temperature / evaporation patterns by state, followed by bushfire trends over time, the native vs plantation burn ratio, and a temperature–burnt-area relationship.
